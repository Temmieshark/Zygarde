Zygarde 10% Forme was revealed in the CoroCoro issue released on September 15th 2015 and is set to be a major player in the upcoming arc of the anime, Pokemon XY & Z. It is one of Zygarde's Complete Forms Zygarde 10% is the Forme that the Zygarde Core takes when it gathers 10% of the Cells nearby. Capable of traveling over 60 mph (100 km/h), it defeats enemies with its sharp fangs and its incredible speed.
