Zygarde! Look its a rarely ever spoken about legendary from generation 6!!

Honestly Zygarde is just a good looking pokemon. He does have good stats, all except his special defence and special attack and speed.

Also taking a look at what its weak to.... It's lacking in some good protection from some well used types.

x4 weakness to Ice! Oh wow. Of course Rock, Poison, and Fire only do 1/2 damage to it. Fairy and Dragon doing x2 damage, and everything else doing normal amounts of damage. I guess its fine... If you dont ever go up against ice types with it.
